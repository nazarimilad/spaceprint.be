De spacemiller

Story

After starting this little 3D-printing hub to bring the 3D printing technologie closer to people in our direct environment we wanted to do something more challenging. We started designing a litlle milling machine that can be upgrated to a 3D-printer or laser machine. Well, this is the result: The Spacemiller. You can download all the stl files you need for free. We also try to give you as much information you need to built our machine. Any questions or comments? Go to our forum page or send us an email. Enjoy!

We started this built without any knowledge of electronics, so don’t be afraid to start the built if you think you don’t have enough skills to do it. We can try to help you as much as we can to finish your built.

The first version of our CNC is ready to built.

To help you with the assembly we included as much foto’s as we could. Though, we are working on an ‘assembly page’.

Part list

Printed parts

If you don’t have a 3D printer, we can print them for you. For the moment we only ship those printed parts in Belgium. You live in another country? Check 3D hubs, maybe you can find someone there who wants to print them for you for a reasonable price.

You can download the stl files from thingiverse


Printing settings:

  • No support needed
  • Printing temp : 215 °C
  • Infill: 40%
  • Printing speed: depends on what your printer can do
